Introduction

What Is OlloCard.com/Activate?

OlloCard.com/Activate is the official online portal provided by Ollo (powered by Continental Finance) for cardholders to activate their new Ollo Mastercard credit card quickly and securely.

Whether you've just received your Ollo Platinum Mastercard or the Ollo Rewards Card, activating it through the official website takes just a few minutes and keeps your financial information protected at every step.

This guide covers everything you need — from prerequisites and the step-by-step activation process to troubleshooting common issues and best security practices.

Step-by-Step

How to Activate Your Ollo Card Online

Follow these simple steps to complete your Ollo Card activation in minutes.

1

Visit the Official Activation Page

Open any web browser and navigate to OlloCard.com/Activate. Make sure the URL begins with https:// — indicating a secure connection.

💡 Bookmark this page for future reference and to avoid phishing websites.
2

Enter Your Card Details

Input the 16-digit card number printed on the front of your Ollo Mastercard. You may also need your card's expiration date and the CVV code on the back.

3

Verify Your Identity

Provide your Social Security Number (last 4 digits), date of birth, and/or the ZIP code used during your card application for identity verification.

🔒 Ollo uses bank-grade 256-bit SSL encryption to protect your personal data.
4

Set Up Your Online Account

If you are a first-time user, create a username and password for your Ollo online account portal. Existing users can simply log in with their credentials.

5

Confirm Activation

Review the details and click "Activate My Card." You will receive a confirmation message on-screen and typically a confirmation email within a few minutes.

✅ Your card is now ready to use for purchases, both in-store and online!
